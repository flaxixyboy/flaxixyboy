Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about my services and process.

What types of projects or apps do you create?

I mainly create Android mobile apps, Windows desktop software (such as custom control panels, media tools), and modern, secure web applications.

Is it a large company or do you work alone?

Flaxixy Boy is a "one-man army" software brand built by myself. From design and coding to deployment—I handle everything perfectly alone. This allows clients to communicate directly with me and get real-time updates on their projects.

You are only 14+, can you handle large projects?

Absolutely! Age is just a number; true identity lies in skill. I have already successfully completed several complex projects involving Firebase databases, custom security systems, and multi-platform apps. You can see the quality of my work in the portfolio section.

What is the typical time and budget for a project?

It entirely depends on the size and features of the project. However, since I don't have the overhead costs of a large company, I can deliver premium quality work within a very affordable budget and the fastest possible time.

Will there be any support or updates after delivery?

Yes, I am always there for any bug fixing or emergency support after the project goes live. Additionally, if you want to add new features to the app or site later, that can also be done.

What is the best way to contact you?

To share a project idea or get a custom quotation, you can message me directly via Telegram, Facebook, or the contact form in the footer. I respond very quickly.
